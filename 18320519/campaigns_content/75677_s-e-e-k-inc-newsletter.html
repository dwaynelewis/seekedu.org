"Back to School Celebration"


"BestBuy/Geek Squad"
 
S.E.E.K, Inc., will host it's first annual, "Back to School Celebration."  This event will provide 50 students in the Over-town community with Drawstring backpacks, pencils, and paper.  BestBuy/ Geek Squad is our  grand sponsor  for  this event.  Geek Squad agents will volunteer their time and pass out school materials to our neighborhood students.

Feed the Homeless

 Our event to feed the homeless will also take place on August 10, 2013.  The items on the menu are:
  • Grilled hamburgers
  • BBQ- Chicken
  • Baked beans
  • Potato salad
  • Deserts
Volunteers
  • All volunteers MUST wear a logo t-shirt during events hosted by S.E. E.K, Inc. (visit www.seekedu.org to purchase a t-shirt)
  • If a shirt is not purchased prior to the event, you can purchase a t-shirts at the event.  However, it is not guaranteed that we will have your size in stock.
  • S.E.E.K, will hosts its first orientation in the month of September to review and clarify rules and regulations.  These orientations will be mandatory for all of S.E.E.K's staff and events.
 Donations
  • Please email us with the date and the amount of money spent on donations, so that you may receive a charitable tax document and the end of our fiscal year.
  • All tangible donations can be brought to the day of the event or 48 hours  prior to the event (contact Program Director).
  • Contact the Program Director for all sponsorships -Anike Sakariyawo | asakariyawo@seekedu.org

 We hope to see you all soon! ~S.E.E.K, Inc.~
