** Volunteers Needed!!
------------------------------------------------------------

It's that time of the year again where we give back to our community in a HUGE way and SEEK, Inc. and our proud sponsors would love for you to join us!

SEEK, Inc. is looking for volunteers from the Miami-Dade & Broward county areas (adults & youth (14-17) to assist with our 3rd Annual "Get Fit & Hip Fair"!

The Fair will be held on Thursday, July 9, 2015 from 10am-3pm at the Miami Beach Police Athletics Field . This year we are expecting a huge turn out and we need your help to ensure EVERYONE enjoys themselves.

Volunteer Positions Available

Event Set-up Crew

Vendor Check In & Set-up

Facilitators

Field Volunteers

Event Break-down Crew

**All volunteer positions will work on a shift schedule**

Shift Schedules

Shift A: 9:30 a.m. - 11:00 a.m.

Shift B: 10:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.

Shift C: 12:30 p.m. - 3:00 p.m.

Shift D: 9:30 a.m. - 3:00 p.m.

Register NOW!
