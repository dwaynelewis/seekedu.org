Announcements


SEEK's "Get Hip and Fit Fair"
1. S.E.E.K presents their second annual health and fitness fair to promote healthy eating, exercise, as well as preventative health care,, which will include fun activities.
2. If you would like to volunteer for different shifts, please register through EventBrite: http://www.eventbrite.com/e/volunteer-for-seeks-2nd-annual-get-hip-fit-fair-registration-13211843981 or contact our Volunteer Coordinator- Ambry Johnson.

Breakthrough Miami and S.E.E.K

1.S.E.E.K.  will continue their collaboration  with Breakthrough Miami throughout the school year for our STEM Program. We are in need of the following materials, such as: Lap tops(technology),and  Robotic materials.
2. Donations can be made  through our website at www.seekedu.org



Volunteers
1. S.E.E.K., Inc., has a new Volunteer Coordinator - Ambry Johnson. All volunteers (new & old) please contact her regarding t-shirts, waivers, applications, etc. She may be contacted at ajohnson@seekedu.org (mailto:ajohnson@seekedu.org) .
2. All volunteers please verify your attendance through Eventbrite:http://www.eventbrite.com/e/volunteer-to-feed-clothe-the-homeless-registration-13212790813
3. All volunteers  MUST ADHERE TO THE DRESS CODE, wear a logo t-shirt or logo apron during events hosted by S.E. E.K, Inc. If you have lost or damage your shirt, you can purchase one from our website (visit www.seekedu.org (http://www.seekedu.org/) ).
4. If a shirt is not purchased prior to the event, you can purchase a t-shirts at the event.  However, it is not guaranteed that we will have your size in stock. Thank you for contacting S.E.E.K, Inc., for your community needs.

Position(s)/Board member(s)

1. S.E.E.K will be taking applications for those individuals who would like to take on a serious position as a board member. This is a non-paying position that requires 2-10 hours, weekly.
2. Opening positions are: Treasury and Grant Writer
3. Applications may be retrieved from our website at www.seekedu.org on the contact page.
