Wheatgrass Park • 100 Palm Street • Saturday • 9am-2pm

This month's market is going to be our best yet! We'll take over Wheatgrass Park from 9am-2pm on Saturday, May 24.

Come by with the family, and stock up on some new items and old favorites. We'll even have a coffee tent and a smoothie station, so you can enjoy some refreshments while you shop.

See you this weekend!

Locally yours,
The Tiller's Market

This month's bounty:


VEGGIES:

Our farmers will be there with collards, kale, spinach, asparagus, onions, mushrooms, turnips, carrots, cauliflower, and Brussels sprouts.

FRUIT:

Now's the time to get your fresh blueberries, peaches, nectarines, and strawberries.

MEAT:

Stop by the meat tent for a few pounds of local grass-fed beef, which is now in the regular rotation. And for the first time ever, we're carrying local sausage. We only have a limited quantity, so show up early to get it.

DAIRY:

We'll have fresh brown eggs and milk by the gallon. Yogurt is back by popular demand, and we're excited to offer creamy goat cheese this month!
Tip: Arrive early for small-batch herbed goat cheese.
