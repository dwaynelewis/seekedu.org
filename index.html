Working To Meet South Florida's Needs

Our Mission

The mission of S.E.E.K is to empower teenagers, parents, teachers, and the community to help Increase access and opportunities for tomorrow’s leaders to build products and learn specific skills through STEM/STEAM Education Programs.

And Vision for South Florida

Our goal is to improve the value of life for children of diverse backgrounds by providing resources that promotes self-advocacy skills, strategic thinking skills, and innovation to become a life long problem solvers.

Learn how you can help

Founded in 2012, SEEK Foundation's mission is to improve the value of life for South Florida's youth. We provide public services, whenever, wherever, and however it is needed to help empower lifelong problem solvers through innovative programs.
