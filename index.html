who we are

We are a mobile non-profit, 501c3 organization who provide services in various communities. Exposing real life situations to our youth, encourages them to lead, assist, and conquer what it means to become a lifelong, productive, problem solver within any setting. Providing children with opportunities to interact with diverse individuals who are less fortunate, will allow them to become more compassionate about the lives of other people. Not only do we believe in the philanthropy of giving back, we also react to needs as they arise, whether it is in our own community or in communities that cross our path.

Vision Statement

Our goal is to improve the value of life for children of diverse backgrounds by providing public services that promote self-advocacy skills, strategic thinking skills, and community involvement to become lifelong problem solvers.

Certified company

SEEK Foundation is a minority, female, certified owned Florida business.

Our experience

Our company is built on trust, transparency and for providing credible resources. We have maintained positive and productive relationships with our clients since 2013.

Mission Statement

The mission of S.E.E.K is to empower teenagers, parents, teachers, and the community to help protect lives and discover educationally diverse programs that promote lifelong problem solvers beyond the classroom.

Volunteers

Our volunteers are committed and passionate about our mission. We connect with individuals throughout Miami Dade and Broward County to create positive change in the lives of others.

Our goal

We aim to provide children with opportunities who are less fortunate to experience the power of being compassionate about the lives of other people.

1 Health and Fitness:

Our fitness education program is designed to help youth in diverse communities become healthier and physically active. We aim to teach the youth about agriculture and how to grow their own food; provide nutrition and fitness education curriculum, online resources, free fitness workshops, fitness classes and outdoor activities.

2 SEEK Inc.

We offer free summer, after school, and Saturday Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEM/STEAM) programs to students in grades K-12. This program engages students with rigorous learning through project based instruction. Our goal is to bridge and associate in-school and out-of-school learning opportunities for learners of the 21st century.
