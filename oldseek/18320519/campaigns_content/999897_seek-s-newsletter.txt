Breakthrough Miami and S.E.E.K

1. S.E.E.K.  will continue their collaboration  with Breakthrough Miami throughout the school year for our STEM Program. The small group of girls will be creating their own organic, cosmetic face, hair and skin products.

New process on how to volunteers for S.E.E.K
1. S.E.E.K.,will start conducting mandatory orientation for all volunteers. Registration is available through our website at   http://www.seekedu.org/Volunteers.html
2. Volunteers can confirm their attendance for each event through Eventbrite on our website at http://www.seekedu.org/Volunteers.html
3. If you have not attend the orientation and you are a former volunteer, please read the volunteer manual (http://www.seekedu.org/Volunteers.html ), read and sign the Volunteer Affidavit,  Acknowledgement & Receipt, and Code of Conduct Certification and Disclosure.
