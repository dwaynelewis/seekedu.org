What is Community Involvement?

Community involvement engages people from all walks of life in dialogue and collaboration with the goal of improving our neighborhood.

Why is it important?

Community involvement is vital because it can improve and or change policies for a better quality of life. It allow our children with opportunities to interact with diverse individuals who are less fortunate, which will allow them to become more compassionate about the lives of other people.

Our Process

Feed and clothe the hungry/homeless, Toy drive, Back to School Give Away, Adopt a Meal Program with Ronald McDonald’s House, and Christmas Give Away.
